Raspberry Mousse Tart

When it's time for dessert, you can't do any better than this Raspberry mousse tart. Made with silken tofu and fresh raspberries, it's the perfect end the perfect meal. It's deliberately light and refreshing, so it can still be enjoyed after the heavier courses are finished. It's one of my favorite desserts and one that I know you'll love!

Ingredients

Crust

  • All purpose flour
  • Coconut oil
  • Almonds
  • Maple Syrup

Mousse

  • Fresh Raspberries
  • Mape Syrup
  • Coconut Cream
  • Cashews (soaked)
