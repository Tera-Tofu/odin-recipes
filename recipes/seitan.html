Delightful Seitan Roast

This roast is going to wow everyone at the table this holiday season! It's moist and flavorful with a crispy skin on the outside that will make you remember the best parts of holiday dinners, without all the worst. It's a bit of prep work, but will be worth every second once you bite into this dish! I hope you like it!

Ingredients

  • Vital Wheat Gluten
  • Nutritional Yeast
  • Yellow Onion
  • Liquid Smoke
  • Sage, dried or Fresh
  • Fresh garlic
  • White wine- for the roast and extra for your glass!
  • Rice paper sheets for the skin
